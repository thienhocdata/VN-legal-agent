# Legal Answer Contract

**Version:** 1.0

**Status:** Approved

**Approved:** 2026-07-15

## Binding decision

Every answer that evaluates whether a concrete legal act is allowed, prohibited, eligible, safe to proceed with, or ready for action must visibly answer six questions:

1. Are the material facts complete?
2. Is there an exception, transitional rule, exclusion or conflict that could change the result?
3. Is each relied-upon provision applicable for the event date and locality?
4. Have all material cross-domain instruments been checked?
5. Is the controlled verdict `ĐƯỢC`, `KHÔNG ĐƯỢC`, or `CHƯA THỂ KẾT LUẬN`?
6. Does every material conclusion have direct provision-level evidence?

The application, not only the model prompt, validates this contract. A conclusive verdict is prohibited without a governed, applicable, provision-level source. Any unresolved material gate forces `CHƯA THỂ KẾT LUẬN`.

Definitions and ordinary conversation do not require the six-part display unless they become a concrete legal decision request.
