# Locality Scope — First Release

**Status:** Approved operating scope  
**Date:** 2026-07-14  
**Primary locality:** Thành phố Hồ Chí Minh  
**Company operating address:** 85 Hưng Nhơn, Thành phố Hồ Chí Minh

## Supported claim

The first release supports controlled preparation and preliminary decision support for common individual or household land-use-right transfer matters in Thành phố Hồ Chí Minh. It combines nationwide transfer-condition research with governed TP.HCM administrative-procedure sources.

## Geographic rule

The Agent normalizes common aliases such as `HCM`, `TPHCM`, `Hồ Chí Minh`, and `Sài Gòn` to `TP. Hồ Chí Minh`.

For Đồng Nai, Tây Ninh, Long An/administrative successors, or any other neighboring locality, the Agent may use verified nationwide law but must not reuse TP.HCM receiving offices, deadlines, forms, fees, or local processes. Those operational details remain unverified until a separate reviewed locality source pack is installed.

## Initial TP.HCM sources

- Law 31/2024/QH15, including Article 45 transfer conditions.
- Law 43/2024/QH15, which changes the relevant effective date to 2024-08-01 subject to the law's stated exceptions.
- Decision 3279/QĐ-UBND dated 2025-06-27, effective 2025-07-01, including procedure 25 for transfer-related land registration.
- Decision 1351/QĐ-UBND dated 2025-09-09, which introduces and modifies land procedures and makes Decision 3279/QĐ-UBND partially ineffective.

Every local procedure output must expose both 3279/QĐ-UBND and the 1351/QĐ-UBND partial-change warning until provision-level change mapping is professionally approved.

## Excluded from supported claim

- Disputes, litigation, complaints, enforcement, fraud, or title certification.
- Organization/project transfers and specialized agricultural, forest, defense, religious, foreign-investment, or vulnerable-party matters.
- Definitive tax, fee, valuation, planning, notarization, or transaction-outcome advice.
- Automatic filing or external action.

These matters may be recorded and prepared only for professional review.
